Welcome to NBA Player Match

A web application that uses a custom map-reduce algorithm to match you to NBA players who play like you!
OR
You can use a machine learning clustering algorithm to group NBA players based on their playstyles!

Click on the links at the top of the page to navigate
The "Match Players" link allows you to match your own playstyle to an NBA player's.
The "Group Players" link allows you to group NBA players based on their playstyles.
The "All Players" link allows you to see the stats of NBA players.
